## 💡 About the Project
**Taqaddam** is an intelligent system powered by predictive data analysis and artificial intelligence[cite: 2]. It is specifically designed to detect hidden academic underperformance early among students[cite: 2]. By moving away from reactive measures, the system provides personalized recommendations to enhance educational equity and improve decision-making quality within the university[cite: 2].

---

## ⚠️ The Problem & Impact

### The Core Challenge
Traditional academic evaluation relies heavily on final GPAs[cite: 2]. This creates a massive lag where student struggles are only discovered *after* a drop in performance has already solidified, preventing early intervention[cite: 2].

### The Impact
* **Delayed Response:** Late academic intervention leaves struggling students without timely assistance[cite: 2].
* **Inaccurate Guidance:** Academic advising decisions are frequently based on outdated, trailing indicators rather than current trends[cite: 2].
* **Increased Risks:** Higher probability of student failure, academic probation, and lost confidence[cite: 2].

---

## ✨ The Solution
Taqaddam offers an innovative, proactive approach to academic support:

* **Predictive Analysis:** Evaluates grade patterns, engagement metrics, and temporal changes to predict risks long before failure occurs[cite: 2].
* **Support Index:** Replaces negative, discouraging classifications with a motivating "Support Index"[cite: 2].
* **Actionable Insights:** Dispatches smart alerts to academic advisors backed by clear data explanations and baseline recommendations[cite: 2].
